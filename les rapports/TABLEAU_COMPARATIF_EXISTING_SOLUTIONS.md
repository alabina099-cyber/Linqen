## Conclusion

Yadulink, Waalaxy, and Dux-Soup all provide LinkedIn automation capabilities, but they differ in scope and architecture:

- **Yadulink** focuses on simple LinkedIn prospecting automation.
- **Waalaxy** offers more advanced multi-channel campaign features, especially LinkedIn and email outreach.
- **Dux-Soup** is lightweight and extension-oriented, but remains strongly dependent on browser execution.

The main common limitation across these tools is that automation reliability can depend on LinkedIn interface stability, browser sessions, pricing plans, and the level of available supervision or customization.
